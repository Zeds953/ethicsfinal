Knowledge Base

The digital world is complex, and understanding how your data is used is the first step toward taking control. The best way to protect yourself and push for better practices is by learning and staying informed.Explore these resources to build a stronger understanding of data governance, privacy, and the ethical issues that come with them.

Insightful Articles & Reports

Data Ethics in 2025: Principles, Frameworks & Key Challenges(

Type: Article | Source: Atlan (atlan.com)

This article provides a detailed overview of data ethics, outlining key principles (like transparency, consent, fairness), a framework for ethical data handling (collection, storage, usage, sharing), and discusses emerging challenges. It's an excellent starting point for understanding the broad scope of data ethics.

Cisco's 2025 Data Privacy Benchmark Study

Type: Report/Study Summary | Source: Cisco Newsroom

This study explores the current data privacy landscape, the business advantages of privacy laws, the role of privacy in responsible AI, and the relationship between data localization and global data flows.

Data privacy and AI: ethical considerations and best practices

Type: Article | Source: TrustCommunity (community.trustcloud.ai)

This source dives into data privacy and artificial intelligence. It covers key ethical considerations such as informed consent, transparency, fairness and non-discrimination in AI algorithms, and outlines best practices for protecting data within AI systems, like data minimization and privacy-by-design.
